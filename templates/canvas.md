# Mimesis Canvas

Use this when a full `.mimesis` loop feels too heavy for the first pass.

Source shape:
Adapted from the neighboring `mimesis-canvas` repository.

## 1. Desired Artifact

What are you trying to create or improve?

## 2. Ten Strong Artifacts

What products, papers, blogs, books, visuals, GitHub READMEs, or user flows define the standard field?

## 3. Knowledge Structure

What are the problem, claim, categories, evidence, and limits?

## 4. Visual Structure

What layout, spacing, hierarchy, color, image use, diagrams, or mobile compression matter?

## 5. Language Rhythm

What sentence length, paragraph rhythm, repeated terms, and density make it work?

## 6. User Flow

How does a first-time reader or user understand and act?

## 7. Taste

Why does it feel good, trusted, fast, deep, or premium?

## 8. Do Not Copy

What brand, sentence, graphic, unique layout, code, or data must not be copied?

## 9. Transform

What should change under your constraints?

## 10. Seven-Day Version

What can you ship and test in seven days?

## Claim Boundary

What can you claim after this pass, and what remains unproven?
