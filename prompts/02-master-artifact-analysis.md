# 02 Master Artifact Analysis

```text
Here are the master artifacts:
[links, screenshots, excerpts, READMEs, papers, landing pages]

Do not summarize them.

Decompose them.

For each artifact, extract:
1. knowledge structure
2. visual structure
3. information hierarchy
4. user flow
5. trust devices
6. proof surfaces
7. language rhythm
8. what makes it feel high-quality
9. what should not be copied

Then compare my current artifact:
[paste or describe current work]

Return:
- what is average about my current version
- what structure I should borrow
- what must be transformed
- what would be plagiarism
- 7-day improvement plan
```
