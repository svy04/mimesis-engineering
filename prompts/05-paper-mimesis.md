# 05 Paper Mimesis

```text
I am writing or evaluating:
[research note, essay, report, technical claim]

Use paper-level mimesis.

Compare against:
[papers, reports, official docs]

Extract:
1. problem statement
2. related work pattern
3. method
4. evaluation
5. limitations
6. threat model
7. claims allowed by evidence

Then rewrite my claim boundaries.
```
