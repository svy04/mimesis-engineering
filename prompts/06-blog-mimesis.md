# 06 Blog Mimesis

```text
I am improving this blog/page:
[link or text]

Use blog-level mimesis.

Compare against:
[blogs, essays, concept hubs, docs]

Analyze:
1. opening promise
2. reading path
3. section order
4. sentence rhythm
5. proof points
6. CTA clarity
7. what feels performative
8. what feels earned

Return:
- remove list
- rewrite list
- missing proof
- next post to publish
```
