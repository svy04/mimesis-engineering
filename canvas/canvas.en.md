# Mimesis Canvas

## 1. Desired Artifact

What are you trying to create or improve?

## 2. Ten Strong Artifacts

Collect products, papers, blogs, books, visuals, GitHub READMEs, or user flows.

## 3. Knowledge Structure

Problem, claim, categories, evidence, limits.

## 4. Visual Structure

Layout, spacing, hierarchy, color, image use, diagrams, mobile compression.

## 5. Language Rhythm

Sentence length, paragraph rhythm, repeated terms, density.

## 6. User Flow

How does a first-time reader or user understand and act?

## 7. Taste

Why does it feel good, trusted, fast, deep, or premium?

## 8. Do Not Copy

Brand, sentence, graphic, unique layout, code, data.

## 9. Transform

What should change under your constraints?

## 10. Seven-Day Version

What can you ship and test in seven days?
