# Mimesis File Protocol

The Mimesis file protocol turns a vague AI workflow into an artifact trail.

If the files do not exist, the loop is not complete.

## Folder Shape

```text
.mimesis/
  spec_lock.md
  procedure_tree.md
  artifact-brief.md
  reference-set.md
  structure-map.md
  transformation-plan.md
  improved-artifact.md
  boundary-check.md
  case-note.md
  run_ledger.md
```

## Required Files

### `spec_lock.md`

Locks the goal, boundaries, forbidden claims, and proof standard.

### `procedure_tree.md`

Defines the steps for the loop.

### `artifact-brief.md`

Names the weak artifact, audience, goal, current weakness, constraints, and proof needed.

### `reference-set.md`

Records the strong artifacts studied, their source type, why they were selected, what to inspect, what not to copy, and what claim boundary applies.

### `structure-map.md`

Extracts knowledge structure, visual structure, user flow, trust devices, claim boundary, failure handling, and rhythm.

### `transformation-plan.md`

Defines what will transfer, what will change, what will be rejected, and what proof is needed.

### `improved-artifact.md`

Stores or links the improved artifact.

### `boundary-check.md`

Inspects surface similarity, wording risk, authority borrowing, proof risk, and attribution.

### `case-note.md`

Records before, references, extracted structure, after, what improved, and what remains unproven.

### `run_ledger.md`

Records what was done and how it was verified.

## Rules

- Do not hide references.
- Do not copy surface.
- Do not invent proof.
- Do not claim outcomes the artifact trail does not support.
- Do not call the loop complete without a case note.

No proof, no claim.
