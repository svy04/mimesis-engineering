# Case Review Checklist

Use this before accepting a public or redacted Mimesis case.

## Intake

- starting artifact is visible or described clearly
- artifact owner is known
- publication preference is explicit
- permission status is explicit
- redaction requirements are explicit

## Mimesis Quality

- references are visible
- extracted structure is named
- transformation is explained
- before/after is present
- originality boundary is checked
- proof boundary is checked

## Safety

- no secrets
- no private customer data
- no copied protected material
- no fake engagement
- no unsupported outcome claim
- no hidden relationship or endorsement

## Publication Decision

Choose one:

- publish
- publish redacted
- keep private
- request more information
- reject

## Boundary

Approving a case means the artifact trail is coherent enough to publish.
It does not prove universal effectiveness, commercial success, legal originality, or customer demand.
