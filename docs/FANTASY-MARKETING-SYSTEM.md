# Fantasy Marketing System

## Definition

Fantasy marketing for Mimesis means creating the feeling that the visitor has found a hidden future work system, while every public claim remains backed by actual artifacts, boundaries, and cases.

Fantasy means mythic positioning backed by real proof.
Fantasy does not mean deception.

## The Desired Feeling

Visitors should feel:

- I have been using AI the old way.
- This names a problem I already felt.
- This is not a prompt trick.
- I can try it on my own artifact.
- I want to be early to this system.

## The Myth

Old world:
People ask AI to pretend.

Hidden truth:
Good work has structure.

New world:
Artifact-first builders feed AI standards.

## The Enemy

Enemy:

## Ethical Guardrails

Do not:

- fake stars
- fake comments
- fake testimonials
- fake users
- fake endorsements
- hide relationships
- overclaim outcomes
- copy surface
- imply proof that does not exist

Do:

- show before/after
- publish proof boundaries
- invite critique
- let users submit cases
- turn confusion into documentation
- let the work create the aura

The fantasy must come from the artifact, not from deception.
